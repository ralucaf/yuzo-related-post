Gets the related post on your blog with any design characteristics.

== Description ==
<h3>Minimalist and Fast..!</h3>

Displays related post your stories with different features of text and image size.

<blockquote><p>Adjusts the image size of post related to fit your template.</p></blockquote>
<h4>This are some of the options Plugin: </h4>
<ul>
<li>Post Type. (post,page,attachment) </li>
<li>Style: Horizontal & Vertical </li>
<li>Categories on which related thumbnails will appear. </li>
<li>Thumbnail size. </li>
<li>Height & Width image. </li>
<li>Background Color. </li>
<li>And more options. </li>
</ul>

Gives shape and life to your post with 'Yuzo related post' ;)

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'Yuzo related post'
3. Activate 'Yuzo related post' from your Plugins page.
4. Visit 'Setting > 'Yuzo related post'.
